Transform how you trade



Layouts and multi-monitor support


Create custom layouts across multiple distinct workspaces or monitors. Name each workspace for easier scanning, then show or hide them to speed up your workflow.

Faster trading

We've optimized the trade tool for fast order entry. It now includes sell short, sell specific, and new trading strategies such as dollar/fractional share trading and price following.

Detailed chart indicators and drawings

Use new menu controls to view categorized indicators and add them to your favorites for easy access.

Increased flexibility and level of detail

Refine your strategy with detailed trend identification using Level 2 quote data, and seamlessly manage and execute complex trades now or save them in ”Saved orders” to execute later.

Create and manage saved orders

"Saved orders" allows you to save an order on the trade ticket and review it from your orders tool later to streamline your strategy and react faster to market opportunities on your own timeline.

View Level 2 quote data

Quickly view the bid and ask prices to gauge market depth and momentum and easily time your next steps. Open an attached time and sales tool as part of the Level 2 quote.

Set and manage alerts

Take informed and decisive action in the dedicated Alerts manager, where you can monitor or add your custom alerts.

New navigation and favorites

Find the tools you need from the redesigned Tools menu, and pin your favorites to the top bar for quick and easy access.

Link tools together

Create up to 8 groups of linked tools across multiple workspaces for faster, data-rich decision-making.

Explore advanced options capabilities



Craft and execute complex orders with multi-leg options and multi-select

  • Choose from preset multi-leg options (MLO) strategies such as buy-write, rolls, spreads (vertical, calendar, diagonal), straddles, and strangles—with more strategies on the way.
  • Follow the current price without manual changes and view real-time options analytics, such as Net Greeks, to discover trading possibilities before you place your trade.
  • Turn on Multi-select in the option chain to pick multiple contracts and assess risk. Then, watch your trade selections automatically populate the trade ticket directly from the chain with one touch.

Build strategies on the chain

Use multi-select to create your own custom strategy and auto-populate your trade—faster.

Calculate theoretical profit and loss

Input a calculation price and date, and modify IV percentage to easily calculate the theoretical profit or loss and probability of a strategy.
