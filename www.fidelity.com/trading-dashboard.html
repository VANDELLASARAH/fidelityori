  • How is Trading Dashboard different from other Fidelity platforms?
    Trading Dashboard is a configurable, web-based platform for seamless monitoring, trading, and managing investments. Unlike the classic Fidelity.com experience, Trading Dashboard offers streaming data and advanced charting. While Active Trader Pro® provides features similar to Trading Dashboard, it requires a PC or Mac download on each device.
  • How can I customize my Trading Dashboard layout?
    You can customize your layout by moving and resizing various tool panels. For example, you could maximize your chart panel, hide your positions, or have them both show simultaneously. Learn more in our step-by-step guide.
  • Do I need to manually save my layout?
    Trading Dashboard automatically saves your layout and the location of your tool panels.
  • What advanced charting features are offered by Trading Dashboard?
    Trading Dashboard offers a wide range of charting tools and indicators, such as VWAP, Bollinger Bands, and moving averages to enhance your trading analysis. Automated pattern recognition helps you spot trends effortlessly, and you can save your charts to keep track of your strategies. You'll also have the ability to quickly trade directly from the chart.
  • What are the system requirements?
    Trading Dashboard is a browser-based tool that has been optimized for larger screens and tablets. It is not intended for use on mobile devices.
